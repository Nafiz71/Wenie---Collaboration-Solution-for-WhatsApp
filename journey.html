üéØ Problem Statement

Users spend excessive time coordinating via WhatsApp, yet conversations rarely lead to concrete outcomes. Key obstacles:

  • Conversational-first architecture: WhatsApp is designed for chat, not structured collaboration.
  • Third-party dependency: Users switch apps (Doodle, Trello, When2Meet) to schedule or decide.
  • Information overload: High message volume creates noise, hiding mission-oriented content.
  • Low actionability: Conversations rarely translate into clear decisions or task completion.
