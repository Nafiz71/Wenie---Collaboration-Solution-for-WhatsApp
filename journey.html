📖 About Wenie ▶️ Try Demo 🚀 Development Journey

🎯 Problem Statement

Users spend excessive time coordinating via WhatsApp, yet conversations rarely lead to concrete outcomes. Key obstacles:

  • Conversational-first architecture: WhatsApp is designed for chat, not structured collaboration.
  • Third-party dependency: Users switch apps (Doodle, Trello, When2Meet) to schedule or decide.
  • Information overload: High message volume creates noise, hiding mission-oriented content.
  • Low actionability: Conversations rarely translate into clear decisions or task completion.

💥 Impact

Time wasted, fragmented workflows, and decision delays

🛣️ My Product Development Roadmap

🔍 Understanding the Problem

  • Gained a customer-centric view and empathy for users
  • Conducted usability interviews and gathered quantitative insights
  • Analyzed average messages to schedule a meeting and time spent scrolling
  • Identified pain points in existing coordination workflows

🤝 Collaboration & Ideation

  • Brainstormed multiple approaches with hypothetical teammates (engineering, design)
  • Converged on single integrated solution addressing multiple collaboration pain points
  • Evaluated technical feasibility and user adoption potential
  • Prioritized features based on impact and implementation complexity

📋 Documentation & Planning

  • Created mini PRD with features, acceptance criteria, and assumptions
  • Defined MVP vs future enhancements for a lean, iterative approach
  • Established phased rollout strategy for risk mitigation
  • Set measurable success criteria and KPIs

🎨 Wireframes & Prototypes

  • Drafted low-fidelity prototypes: poll creation, scheduling interface, AI summaries
  • Iterated designs based on hypothetical usability testing
  • Maximized clarity and minimized friction in user flows
  • Created interactive demo to validate core concepts

📊 Validation & Metrics Planning

  • Planned success metrics: adoption, engagement, efficiency
  • Built feedback loops for continuous improvement post-MVP
  • Designed A/B testing framework for feature optimization
  • Established user research methodology for ongoing insights

💡 Proposed Solution: Wenie AI Assistant

🎯 Core Concept

Wenie is a context-aware AI assistant embedded in WhatsApp to transform chaotic group chats into structured outcomes.

🚀 MVP Features

🗳️

Polls / Decision Making

One-tap voting with deadlines and real-time results

📅

Smart Scheduling

Detects "when are you free?" and suggests mutual availability

✅

Task Assignment

Converts vague responsibilities into actionable tasks

📋

AI Summaries

Condensed highlights of long group conversations

⭐ Key Differentiators

🚀 Zero Setup

Works immediately in existing groups

🧠 Context-Aware

Detects collaboration needs automatically

📱 Platform Native

No switching between apps

🤖 AI-Powered

Learns and improves from usage
